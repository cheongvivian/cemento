    ABOUT IDPM

    IDPM Sdn. Bhd. is a Kuala Lumpur based interior design & build company specializing in corporate offices and space planning. With over 15 years of experience in interior design & construction industries, IDPM has grown by leaps and bound to be where we are today. 
    Our client’s needs and ideas is our priority in turning them into a foundation of the design. We highly value our client’s trust in us, which is why we prioritize our quality and service to meet our client’s ultimate satisfaction.
